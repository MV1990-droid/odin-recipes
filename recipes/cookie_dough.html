You'll need the following ingredients to cook this recipe:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3⁄4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1⁄2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 1⁄2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1⁄2 cup mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  3. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until creamy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add heat-treated flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms. Stir in milk until dough is just combined; fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
